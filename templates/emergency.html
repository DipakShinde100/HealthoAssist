🩹 Basic First Aid Guidelines
🫀 Heart Attack Signs
  • Chest pain or pressure
  • Shortness of breath
  • Pain in arm, jaw, or back
  • Action: Call 112, chew aspirin if available
🧠 Stroke Signs (FAST)
  • Face drooping
  • Arm weakness
  • Speech difficulty
  • Time to call 112
🤒 High Fever (>103°F)
  • Apply cold compress
  • Give paracetamol
  • Keep hydrated
  • Seek medical help if persistent
🩸 Severe Bleeding
  • Apply direct pressure
  • Elevate the wound
  • Do NOT remove embedded objects
  • Call 108 for ambulance
⚠️ Important: In case of any medical emergency, call 112 immediately. Do not rely solely on this application. Always seek professional medical help.
